Superposition

In quantum mechanics, a qubit can be in a combination of states:

$$|\psi\rangle = \frac{|0\rangle + |1\rangle}{\sqrt{2}}$$

from qiskit import QuantumCircuit
qc = QuantumCircuit(1)
qc.h(0)
qc.measure_all()

Entanglement

Entangled qubits share a state no matter the distance.

$$|\Phi^+\rangle = \frac{|00\rangle + |11\rangle}{\sqrt{2}}$$
